LEGAL DECLARATION AND TERMS OF ADMISSION COMPLIANCE

1. GENERAL DECLARATION AND ACCURACY OF INFORMATION 1.1. I, the applicant, hereby certify that all information provided in this application, including all supporting documentation uploaded to the Document Vault, is to the best of my knowledge true, accurate, and complete. 1.2. I acknowledge that the institution reserves the right to cancel my application, withdraw any offer of admission, or terminate my registration at any stage should it be discovered that any information provided is false, misleading, or fraudulent (e.g., fraudulent Matric results or identity documents). 1.3. I understand that "False Info" is a category for immediate rejection and may result in a permanent ban from future applications to this institution.
2. DATA PRIVACY AND PROTECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION (POPIA) 2.1. In accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), I hereby grant consent to the institution to process my personal data, including but not limited to my identity number, contact details, demographic information, and academic records. 2.2. I understand that this data will be used strictly for the purposes of admissions, academic administration, and institutional planning. 2.3. I acknowledge that the institution may verify my academic results (Matric and Post-Schooling) against national databases and previous institutions listed in my academic history.
3. ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS AND SELECTION CRITERIA 3.1. I acknowledge that meeting the minimum Admission Point Score (APS) and specific subject requirements does not guarantee admission. Admission is subject to the availability of space and the institution’s selection ranking. 3.2. I understand that my "1st Choice" is my primary preference and that my "2nd Choice" will only be considered if space is available or if I do not meet the criteria for the primary programme. 3.3. I accept that "Provisional Acceptance" is conditional and is dependent on my final Grade 12 results or the successful completion of currently registered post-school qualifications.
4. DOCUMENTATION AND THE DOCUMENT VAULT 4.1. I agree to ensure that all documents uploaded are clear, legible, and in the required format (PDF/JPEG). 4.2. I understand that if my application status is marked as "MISSING INFO," it is my sole responsibility to monitor the portal and provide the requested documents immediately. 4.3. Failure to provide missing documentation within the specified timeframe may result in my application being marked as "INCOMPLETE" and subsequently discarded.
5. COMMUNICATION AND MONITORING 5.1. I acknowledge that the primary mode of communication regarding my application status is via this Student Portal. 5.2. I agree to check the portal regularly. The institution will not be held liable for missed deadlines or opportunities due to the applicant’s failure to monitor their status (e.g., Pending, Under Review, or Waiting List). 5.3. I confirm that the email address provided during registration is active and that I have secured my account using the mandatory 14-day verification protocol.
6. FINANCIAL LIABILITY AND SPONSORSHIP 6.1. I acknowledge that submission of this application may require an application fee as determined by the current system configuration. 6.2. I understand that providing "Social Grant" or "Sponsor ID" information is required for the assessment of financial support eligibility but does not constitute an automatic grant of funding.
7. DISABILITY AND SPECIAL ACCOMMODATIONS 7.1. If I have declared a disability, I understand that the information provided will be used to assess the institution's capacity to provide reasonable accommodation for my studies. 7.2. I certify that the details provided in the disability section are comprehensive and intended to facilitate my academic success.
8. RULES AND REGULATIONS 8.1. By clicking "Submit," I agree to abide by the rules, regulations, and policies of the institution as set out in the Student Handbook and the Institutional Statute. 8.2. I acknowledge that this application is a legally binding commitment to the terms mentioned above.
