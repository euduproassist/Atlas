CONSENT FOR THIRD-PARTY DATA SHARING AND PROCESSING

​1. PREAMBLE AND SCOPE In accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), No. 4 of 2013, and other applicable data protection legislations in South Africa, this institution ("the Institution") requires your explicit consent to share your personal, academic, and demographic data with verified third-party service providers. This consent is a prerequisite for facilitating essential student support services including, but not limited to, external career placements, off-campus housing, and financial aid processing. ​2. NATURE OF DATA TO BE SHARED By accepting this agreement, you authorize the Institution to disclose the following categories of information as captured in your digital application profile: ​Personal Identifiers: Full names, Identity/Passport number, and student number. ​Contact Details: Mobile number, email address, and physical residential address. ​Academic Records: Current qualification, module percentages, year of study, and Admission Point Score (APS). ​Demographic Data: Race, gender, and disability status (where required for statutory reporting or specialized support). ​Documentation: Digital copies of ID documents, academic transcripts, and matric certificates stored in the Institution’s Document Vault. ​3. AUTHORIZED THIRD-PARTY RECIPIENTS Data will only be shared with entities that have entered into formal Data Processing Agreements with the Institution, ensuring they maintain equivalent security standards. These include: ​Career and Recruitment Portals: For the purposes of work-integrated learning (WIL), internships, bursary applications, and graduate recruitment. ​Accredited Housing Providers: To facilitate applications for off-campus student accommodation and private residential leases. ​Financial Institutions and Regulatory Bodies: Including NSFAS, scholarship donors, and credit bureaus for fee-funding verification. ​Service Providers: Technical partners managing the cloud infrastructure, Firebase authentication, and payment gateways (e.g., Paystack). ​4. PURPOSE OF PROCESSING The sharing of data is conducted strictly for the following purposes: ​To enable external parties to verify your enrollment status and academic eligibility. ​To streamline the transition from academic study to professional employment. ​To ensure student safety and administrative compliance in off-campus housing. ​To fulfill statutory reporting requirements to the Department of Higher Education and Training (DHET). ​5. DATA SECURITY AND RETENTION The Institution employs industry-standard encryption and Firebase security rules to protect your data during transit and at rest. Third parties are contractually prohibited from using your data for marketing purposes or further distribution without additional consent. Your data will be retained for the duration of your academic lifecycle and for the minimum period required by the National Archives and Records Service of South Africa Act. ​6. STUDENT RIGHTS AND WITHDRAWAL OF CONSENT You have the right to: ​Request access to the record of third parties who have received your data. ​Object to the processing of your personal information for specific non-essential purposes. ​Request the correction or deletion of inaccurate data. ​Withdraw this consent at any time by notifying the Registrar’s Office in writing, acknowledging that such withdrawal may result in the termination of access to certain institutional and third-party services (e.g., housing or career placements). ​7. DECLARATION AND ACCEPTANCE I, the undersigned applicant, hereby grant the Institution permission to share my personal and academic information with authorized third-party providers as outlined above. I confirm that all information provided in my application is true and correct, and I understand that providing false information may result in the immediate cancellation of my application or registration.
